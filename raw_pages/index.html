E.g.

  • 万有引力定律

    $$ F = G \frac{mm'}{r^{2}}$$

  • 麦克斯韦方程组

    $$ \nabla \times H = J + \frac{\partial D}{\partial t}$$ $$ \nabla \times E = - \frac{\partial B}{\partial t}$$ $$ \nabla\cdot B = 0 $$ $$ \nabla\cdot D = \rho$$ 其中,\(\nabla \times\)即\(\vec{\nabla} \times\)矢量场旋度.

  • 海森伯不确定性原理

    $$ [\Delta x] \cdot [\Delta v] \leq \frac{h}{m}$$ 其中,\(h\)是普朗克常量, \(m\)是质量.

  • 相对论性

    $$ m = \frac{m_{0}}{\sqrt{1-\frac{v^2}{c^2}}}$$ 其中,\(m_{0}\)是静止质量,c为光速,v为速度。质量随速度变化。

    The Quadratic Formula

    \[x = {-b \pm \sqrt{b^2-4ac} \over 2a}.\]

    Cauchy's Integral Formula

    \[f(a) = \frac{1}{2\pi i} \oint\frac{f(z)}{z-a}dz\]

    Angle Sum Formula for Cosines

    \[ \cos(\theta+\phi)=\cos(\theta)\cos(\phi)−\sin(\theta)\sin(\phi) \]

    Gauss' Divergence Theorem 散度

    \[ \int_D ({\nabla\cdot} F)dV=\int_{\partial D} F\cdot ndS \]

    Curl of a Vector Field 旋度

    \[ \vec{\nabla} \times \vec{F} = \left( \frac{\partial F_z}{\partial y} - \frac{\partial F_y}{\partial z} \right) \mathbf{i} + \left( \frac{\partial F_x}{\partial z} - \frac{\partial F_z}{\partial x} \right) \mathbf{j} + \left( \frac{\partial F_y}{\partial x} - \frac{\partial F_x}{\partial y} \right) \mathbf{k} \]

    Standard Deviation

    \[\sigma = \sqrt{ \frac{1}{N} \sum_{i=1}^N (x_i -\mu)^2} \]

    \[(\nabla_X Y)^k = X^i (\nabla_i Y)^k = X^i \left( \frac{\partial Y^k}{\partial x^i} + \Gamma_{im}^k Y^m \right)\]
